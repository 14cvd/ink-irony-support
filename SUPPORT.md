### ❓ Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

**Q: Do I need an internet connection to play?**
A: **No!** Ink & Irony is a 100% offline premium experience. You can play the game, access all 5 languages, and get mocked by our taunt system without ever connecting to Wi-Fi or cellular data.

**Q: Why doesn't the timer work on Easy mode?**
A: That is by design! "Easy" difficulty offers infinite time and generous lives (8 mistakes allowed) so you can learn the words at your own pace. If you want the timer, try Medium, Hard, or Nightmare difficulty.

**Q: Will you add more languages or words?**
A: Yes! We are constantly working on expanding our localized dictionaries. Currently, we support English, Spanish, Turkish, Russian, and Azerbaijani. If you have a specific language request, let us know!

**Q: My high score or streak reset. What happened?**
A: The game saves your progress securely on your local device. If you uninstall and reinstall the app, your local data (including streaks and leaderboards) will be permanently deleted. We do not store your data on external servers for your privacy.

**Q: How do the offline AI taunts work?**
A: The game uses a massive built-in dictionary of localized, context-aware insults. The more you fail, the more savage the game becomes. No internet required—just thick skin!
